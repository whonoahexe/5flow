Regulatory requirements vary by region and product category, but a few principles apply everywhere:

- Keep ingredient lists accurate and readable
- Substantiate every claim with documented evidence
- Localize labels for language and legal nuances
- Maintain audit trails across the lifecycle

Start small: define who owns each compliance step, collect reusable proofs, and templatize mandatory elements to reduce errors.
